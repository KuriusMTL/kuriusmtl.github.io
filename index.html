What is Kurius?

Kurius is a start-up non-profit organization created by students for students. Our mission is to provide free, accessible and quality tech education for all students in Montreal by working and partnering up with world-class organizations, companies, mentors and educators.

Introducing: Kurius Hack

Kurius Hack

Our program is a 5-week long "hack" during which participants learn to code by tackling a specific problem in a guided workshop experience, divided into Kurius Capsules and Kurius Workshops.Each Kurius Hack will revolve around a specific theme such as website development and cryptocurrency. Our first Kurius Hack will be a Kurius Web Hack.

Kurius Capsules

Specially designed for our students in mind, our Capsules gather a variety of industry specialists in a weekly workshop session. They have the unique chance to learn from the best in a tight-nit community of equally passionate students.

Kurius Periods

Specially designed for our students in mind, our Periods allow our participants to collaborate in a fun and engaging session dedicated to coding during a weekend, and work together on a big project that will be showcased at the end of the Kurius Hack.
