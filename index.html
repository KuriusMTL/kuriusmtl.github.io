What is Kurius?

Kurius is a start-up non-profit organization created by students for students. Our mission is to provide free, accessible and quality tech education for all students in Montreal by working and partnering up with world-class organizations, companies, mentors and educators.

What do we do?

Kurius organizes numerous events and programs throughout the city of Montreal in order to enable the youth to change the world with tech. Currently, we are working on a new and exciting project: Kurius Hack. Our hope is to deploy Kurius Hack in numerous schools across Montreal, with around 15-25 participants for our first pilot project, and possibly expanding to more than 50 participants per Kurius Hack if our pilot project shows great success.

Kurius Web Hack

Our program is a 5-week long "hack" during which participants learn to code by tackling a specific problem in a guided workshop experience, divided into Kurius Capsules and Kurius Periods. Each Kurius Hack will revolve around a specific themesuch as website development and cryptocurrency. Our first Kurius Hack will be a Kurius Web Hack.

Kurius Capsules

Specially designed for our students in mind, our Capsules gather a variety of industry specialists in a weekly workshop session. They have the unique chance to learn from the best in a tight-nit community of equally passionate students.

Kurius Periods

Specially designed for our students in mind, our Periods allow our participants to collaborate in a fun and engaging session dedicated to coding during a weekend, and work together on a big project that will be showcased at the end of the Kurius Hack.
