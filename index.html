Who We Are

We are a start-up non-profit organization created by students for students. Our goal is to empower youth by making tech education and resources accessible to all.

Kurius Capsules

Specially designed for our students in mind, our Capsules gather a variety of industry specialists in a weekly workshop session. They have the unique chance to learn from the best in a tight-nit community of equally passionate students.

Kurius Periods

What Is a Kurius Hack?

Our program is a 5-week long "hack" during which participants learn to code by tackling a specific problem in a guided workshop experience. They are held twice a week: once after school and once during the weekend.
